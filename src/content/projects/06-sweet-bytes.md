Brand identity and ordering flow for a small Austin bakery that had been using a third-party platform with bad UX and high fees. Built a custom ordering system in Figma, then handed off to a developer friend to implement.

Designed a full visual system: wordmark, icon, color palette (warm creams and terracotta), custom illustration style for the product photography backgrounds, and a pattern library. The bakery's Instagram following grew by 40% in the first month after the rebrand, which the owner credits partly to having cohesive visual assets for the first time.
