A data dashboard for a local Austin nonprofit that tracks volunteer hours, event attendance, and impact metrics. The client had been sharing a static spreadsheet with their board — the goal was to make the data actually readable and engaging.

Designed in Figma, built in React. Used a pastel color system to soften the data density. Added small animations on hover that make the numbers feel alive. The nonprofit saw a 3x increase in volunteer sign-ups in the month after launch, which they partly attribute to board members finally understanding the impact visually.
