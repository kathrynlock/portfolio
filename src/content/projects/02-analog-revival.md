Mixed-methods research project combining ethnographic interviews (n=24) with a quantitative survey (n=340). The central finding: people aren't choosing analog for nostalgia — they're choosing it for controllability. Digital tools feel ambient and demanding; analog tools feel bounded and personal.

Published as a 40-page research report. Presented at the UT Undergraduate Research Forum. Currently revising for journal submission.
